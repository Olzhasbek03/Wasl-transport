Learn About Our Vision

A Disconnected Journey

For students and staff in large communities like Qatar Foundation, daily transportation is a source of frustration, impacting productivity and well-being.

Unpredictable Wait Times

A lack of real-time tracking on existing shuttles leads to uncertainty and wasted time.

Inefficient Routes

Fixed routes don't cater to individual needs, resulting in unnecessarily long travel times.

Lack of On-Demand

No system exists for requesting a ride precisely when and where it's needed, especially after hours.

Safety Concerns

Walking alone at night is a significant concern that a reliable, trackable service can solve.

The Solution: Smart, On-Demand Mobility

Wasl Transport provides a seamless, Uber-like experience designed for the modern campus, with features built for reliability and peace of mind.

On-Demand Rides

Request a shuttle from anywhere to anywhere within the service area. No more waiting for fixed-route buses.

Real-Time Tracking

See exactly where your shuttle is on the map and get precise, dynamically updated ETAs.

Smart Routing

Our system optimizes routes in real-time to efficiently pick up multiple passengers, ensuring the fastest trip for everyone.

Enhanced Safety

Know your driver's name, vehicle details, and track your entire journey in the app for complete peace of mind.

Our Vision: Beyond the Campus

Wasl Transport is the launchpad for a fully integrated public transportation platform for Qatar, creating a single source of truth for all transit.

1

Phase 1: Perfect the Model in QF
